Technical Sourcing

Technical sourcing is the backbone of modern recruitment. It focuses on proactively identifying candidates rather than waiting for applications. Recruiters use platforms like LinkedIn, GitHub, and online communities to discover top talent efficiently.

Boolean Search Techniques
