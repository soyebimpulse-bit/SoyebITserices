How Vistar Evaluates an Image

Every submission enters our blind review pipeline. Three independent specialists score each image on four criteria, and only those averaging 85/100 or higher across all axes receive Vistar Certification. The process is meticulous, fair, and never rushed — because mediocre images have no place in our collection.

S
Sharpness
Technical excellence, focus accuracy, dynamic range, colour fidelity, and pixel-level integrity.
T
Tone
Mood, emotional register, narrative depth, and the image's ability to transport the viewer.
A
Authenticity
Original creative vision, absence of deceptive manipulation, genuine cultural representation.
R
Resonance
Lasting visual impact, cultural significance, and breadth of commercial or artistic applicability.
