Boolean Search Techniques

("Java Developer" OR "Backend Engineer") AND (Spring OR Microservices) NOT Junior
("DevOps Engineer" OR "SRE") AND AWS AND Kubernetes
Boolean search helps you filter candidates with precision and speed.

Sourcing Expert Tips

Strong sourcing goes beyond standard job titles by using synonyms like SWE or Backend Developer to expand results. Combining Boolean search with niche keywords helps uncover hidden talent across platforms such as LinkedIn, GitHub, and Stack Overflow. Understanding acronyms and building a clear candidate profile ensures better targeting, while focusing on both technical and soft skills improves quality. Expanding channels, leveraging referrals, and engaging in tech communities increases reach and attracts passive candidates. Offering remote flexibility broadens the talent pool, while personalised outreach based on real work improves response rates. In a fast-moving market, speed matters, and showcasing your company’s impact helps secure top talent.
